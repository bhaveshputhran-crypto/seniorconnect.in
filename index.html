Our Story

Built From Personal Experience, Driven by Purpose

SeniorConnect was born from a shared realization — five friends who watched their own grandparents struggle with loneliness, confusion, and daily challenges while family members were far away, busy, or living abroad.

Each founder had felt the helplessness of being unable to be physically present for the elders they loved. They asked themselves: Why should distance mean neglect? Why should seniors age without dignity and company?

SeniorConnect was their answer — India's most trusted elder companionship platform, starting in Delhi NCR and expanding across the country.

How It Works

Six Simple Steps to Peace of Mind

From first contact to ongoing care — our process is designed to be easy, personal, and completely reassuring.

1

Family Signs Up

Create your free account on the SeniorConnect app or website. Tell us a little about your parent or grandparent, where they live, and what kind of support they might need.

2

Describe Your Senior's Needs

Fill in a simple needs assessment — daily routine, health conditions, preferred activities, language, and the kind of companionship that would matter most. The more you share, the better we match.

3

We Match the Perfect Companion

Our team reviews your senior's profile and selects the most compatible verified companion nearby — considering location, language, personality, and specific requirements.

4

Introductory Visit

The companion visits your senior (with a SeniorConnect coordinator if needed) for a free introductory meeting. You join via video call from anywhere. If the match feels right, we proceed.

5

Regular Visits Begin

Your companion starts their scheduled visits — bringing warmth, help, and presence into your senior's daily life. Every visit is logged and shared with your family dashboard.

6

Family Stays Updated

Receive real-time updates, visit summaries, health observations, and videos through the app. Reach your companion anytime. Your peace of mind is always just a tap away.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Are all SeniorConnect companions background-verified?

Yes. Every companion undergoes a comprehensive police verification, identity check, and reference verification before joining our platform. We also provide mandatory training. Your loved one's safety is our highest priority.

How quickly can I get a companion arranged?

In most cases, we can arrange an introductory visit within 48–72 hours of your signup. For urgent situations, same-day or next-day arrangements may be possible depending on availability in your area.

Can I choose or change my companion?

Absolutely. We always start with an introductory meeting, and if the match doesn't feel right, we'll find another companion at no extra charge. Ongoing, you can request a change at any time through the app.

How do I know what happened during each visit?

After every visit, your companion submits a detailed note through the app — covering what they did, how your senior was feeling, any concerns, and highlights. For Premium and NRI plans, photo and video updates are included.

Is SeniorConnect available outside Delhi NCR?

We're currently live in Delhi NCR, including Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad. Expansion to Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, and other major cities is planned for 2025.

Can I pay from abroad as an NRI?

Yes, absolutely. We accept all major international cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Our NRI Care Plan is specifically designed for families living abroad, with a dedicated India-based coordinator as your point of contact.

What if there is an emergency?

All companions are trained in basic emergency response. Our emergency check-in line is available 24×7. In a serious situation, companions will immediately alert local emergency services, contact family via the app, and coordinate until help arrives.

Can I cancel or pause my plan?

Yes. There are no lock-in contracts. You can pause or cancel your plan anytime through the app with no penalties. We ask for 3 days notice for scheduling purposes.
